College Level Avocado Toast, April 7, 2020

Instructions

The first step in making avocado toast is to gather your ingredients.

After, cut avocado in half and take out pit.

Toast bread to desired level.

Scoop out avocado filling and spread on toast to desired amount.

Sprinkle the desired amount of everything bagel seasoning, salt. Add paprika after.

Lastly, enjoy! I hope you love this twist that makes avocado toast more exciting.
